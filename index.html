Artifact 2: Whitespace Remover

The second artifact focused on a topic I’ve been personally interested in for some time, file compression and encryption. I’ve always wanted to deepen my understanding of how these processes work, and this project allowed me to take a meaningful first step in that direction. I designed a program that reads input from a file, processes the content by identifying and removing white spaces, and then writes the cleaned output to a new file. This experience helped me grasp foundational concepts related to data handling and optimization.

The final artifact was created to demonstrate my understanding of working with databases. This project highlights my growing proficiency in backend development and cloud-based data management.
